About

Hello!

I'm a game designer with experience working in both large-scale AAA games, as well as smaller projects where I had the opportunity to engage with every aspect.

I have a particular interest in systems design, and how games evolve from many interconnected systems to become intricate (and fragile!) structures.

I'm also eager to learn and enjoy exploring other aspects of game development. This has especially led to developing a strong technical foundation which helps me in understanding and designing complex game systems.
